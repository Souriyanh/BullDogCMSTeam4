Published on Jan 11, 2017

Could you spot a victim of trafficking in a crowd? Probably not — victims may not look any different than anyone else you see every day. This video introduces you to the HHS Look Beneath the Surface campaign. Through this campaign HHS works with grantees and stakeholders to raise awareness of human trafficking and the factors that make certain populations more at risk.

The campaign also empowers the public to help identify victims and connect those victims with services by calling the National Human Trafficking Hotline at 888-373-7888 or visiting https://humantraffickinghotline.org.
